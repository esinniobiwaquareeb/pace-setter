Privacy Policy

Pace Setter Cleaning Services LTD respects your privacy. This page explains how information submitted through the website may be handled.

Information We Receive

When you use the booking form, you may provide your name, email address, phone number, property details, and other enquiry information.

How We Use It

  • To review your cleaning enquiry
  • To respond by email, phone, or WhatsApp
  • To arrange quotes, bookings, and follow-up communication

Local Storage

This website may save recent enquiry details locally on your device to improve the booking experience. That local storage can be cleared from your browser at any time.

Third-Party Services

Using the booking flow may open WhatsApp or your email application. Their own privacy policies will apply once you leave this site.
